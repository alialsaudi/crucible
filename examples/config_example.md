## 1. YOUR IDEA

```
A mobile app for Qatar that turns commercial streets into explorable 3D environments.
Each shop is a navigable digital twin built from LiDAR stitched photography with AI-indexed
products. Serves two users: bored browsers who want an immersive local discovery experience
(like walking around GTA but real), and desperate searchers who need to find a specific item
at a nearby shop RIGHT NOW. Shops are onboarded by scanning with iPhone LiDAR. AI indexes
all visible products — no manual data entry needed. Search is rapid, multilingual (Arabic +
English), and works like Apple Photos text recognition but across all shop inventory. Revenue
from shop subscriptions, delivery partnerships (Talabat/Snoonu), and potentially B2B licensing.
```
